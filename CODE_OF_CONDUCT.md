# Code of Conduct

Be direct, constructive, and respectful.

Disagreement is allowed and useful. Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, doxxing, spam, and deliberate disruption are not.

Review ideas and contributions on their evidence, clarity, usefulness, and risks. Criticism should identify the problem and, where practical, suggest a better approach.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
